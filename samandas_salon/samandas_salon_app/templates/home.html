About the salon

Here is a brief introduction on us and what we offer.

Located in the village of Potter Heigham, Samandas Salon is a little family run salon, that has progressed beautifully from a room in the conservatory to an ever-expanding multi-room salon, providing a luxurious spa atmosphere to our clients. As a small business you'll have the luxury of knowing you will be the only client in your appointment (unless you choose to bring a friend) so you have peace of mind knowing your time will be quiet, calm and all about you.
